Instructions

Dear participant , thanks again for agreeing to participate in this experiment.

Please take note of the following instructions on how to use this interface for the experiment, as well as the procedures involved.


Interface

After this instruction page, you will be testing out 2 types of copy-pasting techniques, namely:

  • Keyboard Shortcuts
  • AutoComPaste

On the screen, you will see the trial features, followed by the instructions to copy-paste a certain text using the specified technique.

On the main canvas, you will observe multiple article windows, each filled with some text. Your objective is to locate the specified text (in red, on left panel) among the articles, either by visually scanning for a highlighted text (in yellow), or by typing in the editor window for a dropdown of suggestions.

Please note that the former (finding the highlighted text) is reserved for the keyboard shortcut copy-pasting technique, and the latter is only available when you are copy-pasting using the AutoComPaste technique.

Once you are done, click the "Next Task" button (in blue, on the left panel) to proceed to the next page.


Structure Of Trials

After every 18 questions (the end of a series of trials on a single technique), you will be required to take a 1-minute break. After which, you will repeat the technique-series again. This will repeat for a second technique, which brings it to 3 break intervals and 72 trial questions.

You may notice that at some point in the trial, you will be asked to copy different length of text, or that the articles have changed to a different set. This is part of our experiment on different factors affecting the copy-pasting experience.

After the trials, you will be required to fill up a post-experiment questionnaire! It will help us tremendously to give your honest input on our experiment!


How-To Use Copy-Paste Techniques

Keyboard Shortcut

Using your mouse, select the text you wish to copy, then press (CTRL or CMD) + C. Then, select the editor space and press (CTRL or CMD) + V.

AutoComPaste

Select the editor space, then proceed to type the stimuli text prompted in red. You will then observe a dropdown of suggested text. Take your pick and modify if necessary.
